Saniya enterprises is a trusted name in the polybag manufacturing industry, dedicated to providing high-quality, durable, and eco-conscious plastic packaging solutions. Established with a vision to meet the growing demand for flexible packaging, we specialize in the production of LDPE, HDPE, PP, and biodegradable polybags for a wide range of applications across retail, food, agriculture, e-commerce, and industrial sectors. Our manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced blown film extrusion, flexographic/rotogravure printing, and cutting and sealing machines, enabling us to deliver customized products with precision and efficiency. From plain carry bags to custom-printed courier and garment bags, we offer a complete packaging solution tailored to our clients’ needs. At Saniya enterprises, we place a strong emphasis on quality control, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We follow strict environmental guidelines and are committed to using recyclable and biodegradable materials that reduce plastic waste and promote a cleaner future. Driven by innovation, reliability, and a customer-first mindset, we aim to become a leading manufacturer and exporter in the flexible packaging domain.
